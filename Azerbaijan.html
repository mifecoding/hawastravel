Trip Overview

Discover the perfect combination of Baku and Gabala, two gems of this fascinating country. In Baku, explore the old fortified city, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with its picturesque streets and historic monuments. Soak up the city's cosmopolitan atmosphere by visiting its modern neighborhoods and strolling along the lively promenade. Then, head to Gabala, where nature reigns supreme. Admire the breathtaking landscapes of mountains, lush forests, and crystal-clear lakes. Indulge in the outdoor activities.

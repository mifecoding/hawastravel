About Us

At HAWAS, we’re revolutionizing the way travelers discover and book tours. As an online marketplace, we don’t create or run tours ourselves—instead, we connect adventurers with trusted third-party tour operators worldwide.
Much like Amazon for shopping or Airbnb for accommodations, HAWAS serves as a one-stop platform where travelers can explore and compare thousands of tours from independent providers. Our model benefits both sides of the travel experience:
For travelers: We offer a seamless booking process with access to a vast selection of tours, complete with comparison tools to find the perfect experience.
For tour operators: We provide global visibility, helping businesses grow by reaching a wider audience without the need for costly marketing.

By digitizing the traditional travel agency model, we eliminate the hassle of in-person visits and outdated booking methods. With HAWAS, planning your next adventure is easier, faster, and more convenient than ever.
